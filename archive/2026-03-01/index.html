grab the bull by the horns
/ɡræb ðə bʊl baɪ ðə hɔrnz/
أمسك الثور من قرونه
📖 الشرح

💬 أمثلة على الاستخدام
When the project was falling behind, I decided to grab the bull by the horns and talk to my manager.
She had to grab the bull by the horns and confront her fears about public speaking.
If you want to improve your health, you need to grab the bull by the horns and start exercising regularly.
